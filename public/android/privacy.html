Go Live Simulator Privacy Policy (Android)

Effective Date: June 19, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 24, 2026  ·  Applies to the Android app com.golivesimulator.app

1. Who We Are

Go Live Simulator is operated by DigiTech Dynamics LLC, based in Minnesota, United States. We are the data controller for the information described in this policy. You can reach us anytime at team@digitechdynamics.com.

2. What Go Live Is (and Isn't)

Go Live is a simulator. There is no real audience, no public broadcast, and no other users watching you. Every viewer, comment, and reaction is generated by artificial intelligence on a private, single-player session. Nothing you do in Go Live is ever published or shared with other Go Live users.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information you give us directly

  • Account details — username, age and gender (if you choose to share them), profile picture, bio, and any preferences you set.
  • Subscription status — handled by Google Play Billing and our processor RevenueCat. We do not see your payment-card numbers.

3.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device data — device model, Android version, language, and a Firebase Installation ID.
  • Advertising ID (GAID) — your Google Advertising ID, used for ads, unless you opt out in your device settings.
  • Crash and diagnostics data — via Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Product analytics — events like screen views, button taps, and stream session length via Mixpanel and Firebase Analytics.

3.3 Information processed when you stream or message

To power the simulated AI audience, comments, direct messages, and recaps, Go Live sends data to third-party AI providers that act as our processors:

  • Microphone audio → Deepgram. While a stream is active, audio captured from your microphone is streamed to Deepgram, which converts your speech into a text transcript in real time.
  • Text → Anthropic (Claude) and Google (Gemini). Text — including the speech transcript above, messages you type, your username and bio, and a short summary of recent on-screen activity — is sent to Anthropic and/or Google to generate the simulated comments, direct-message replies, and stream recaps.

Your camera is used only to show a live preview on your own screen. Camera images are not transmitted to us or to any third party, and are not stored. Microphone audio is transmitted only while a stream is actively running; when you stop the stream, transmission stops immediately. We do not save your audio, transcripts, or message text to our servers.

5. AI Provider Disclosure

The simulated audience comments, direct messages, and recaps in Go Live are generated by third-party AI services that act as our processors:

  • Google Gemini API — generates simulated audience comments and guest responses from text input.
  • Anthropic (Claude) — generates direct-message replies, persona comments, and stream recaps from text input (including your username, bio, and messages you type).
  • Deepgram — transcribes your microphone audio into text while you stream.

We use the paid API tiers of these services. Under their respective API terms, your data is not used to train their AI models. Providers may briefly log request data for abuse-detection purposes only. See Google's Gemini API Terms, Anthropic's Commercial Terms, and Deepgram's Privacy Policy for details.

AI-generated reactions are core to how Go Live works. You can stop microphone transmission at any time by ending your stream. If you do not want to use AI features, do not start a stream or send messages.

6. How We Use Your Information

  • To run the app and generate the AI audience, messages, and recaps in real time.
  • To process and verify your subscription with Google Play and RevenueCat.
  • To understand how the app is used, fix crashes, and improve features.
  • To deliver and measure ads via Google AdMob.

8. Data We Do Not Collect

  • Health, fitness, or medical data.
  • Precise GPS or background location.
  • Contacts, calendar, or browsing history.
  • Your camera images — these stay on your device for preview only and are never transmitted or stored.
  • Your raw microphone feed for storage — audio is streamed to Deepgram for transcription in real time and is not retained.

9. Google Play Data Safety

For transparency, here is how Go Live's data practices map to the categories in our Google Play Data safety declaration:

Data Collected

  • Personal identifiers — an app-generated user ID, app preferences.
  • App activity — in-app interactions and screen views.
  • App info and performance — crash logs and diagnostics.
  • Device or other IDs — Advertising ID, Firebase Installation ID.
  • Financial info — purchase history (subscription / coin packs).

Data Processed but Not Stored by Us

  • Audio — microphone audio is sent to Deepgram for real-time transcription during active streams and is not retained by us.
  • Messages and other text — message text, your username and bio, and stream context are sent to Anthropic and Google to generate AI responses, and are not retained by us.

Purposes

  • App functionality, analytics, and advertising.

Camera images are processed on-device only and are not collected. Data is encrypted in transit, and you can request deletion at any time (see Section 10).

10. Your Rights and Choices

10.1 Delete your account

In the app, go to Settings → Delete Account, or visit our Account Deletion page. This deletes your RevenueCat customer record, Mixpanel profile, analytics identifiers, and all on-device data within 30 days. You can also email team@digitechdynamics.com to request deletion.

10.2 Manage your subscription

Open the Google Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions.

10.3 Opt out of ad personalization

In Android Settings → Privacy / Ads, you can delete or reset your Advertising ID and opt out of ad personalization.

10.4 GDPR rights (EEA, UK, Switzerland)

You can request access, rectification, erasure, portability, restriction, or object to processing. Email team@digitechdynamics.com. We rely on legitimate interest for analytics, contractual necessity for the subscription, and consent for advertising-related processing.

10.5 CCPA / CPRA rights (California)

You have the right to know what we collect, to delete it, to correct it, to opt out of any sale or sharing of personal information, and not to be discriminated against for exercising your rights. We do not sell personal information. Some advertising activity may qualify as "sharing" under CPRA; you can opt out by resetting your Advertising ID or by emailing us.

11. Data Retention

Account data and analytics are retained while your account is active. After you delete your account (or request deletion by email), we delete your account record and analytics profile within 30 days, subject to short backup-retention periods that auto-expire. Microphone audio sent to Deepgram, and text sent to Anthropic and Google, during sessions is processed in real time and is not retained by us beyond the request.

12. Security

All network traffic is encrypted using TLS. Go Live does not require you to create an account or sign in, and we do not collect passwords; your data is associated with an app-generated identifier stored on your device.

13. International Transfers

Our processors (Firebase, RevenueCat, Mixpanel, Google AdMob, Google Gemini API, Anthropic, Deepgram, Google Play) operate primarily in the United States. If you use Go Live from outside the United States, your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States and potentially other countries where these providers operate.

14. Children's Privacy

Go Live is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA/UK). If a parent or guardian believes a child has provided personal information in Go Live, please email team@digitechdynamics.com and we will delete it.

15. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app before the change takes effect.
