Delete Your Go Live Simulator Account & Data

Applies to the Go Live Simulator app by DigiTech Dynamics LLC

You can request deletion of your Go Live Simulator account and its associated data at any time, using either of the methods below. This page explains exactly what is deleted, what may be retained, and how long it takes.

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app

The fastest way is directly in the app, where we can identify your account automatically:

Open Settings → Delete Account, then confirm. Your account and on-device data are removed immediately, and the rest of your data is purged from our systems within 30 days.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you have already uninstalled the app, email us and we will delete your account and data for you. Because Go Live accounts are anonymous, please include your User ID if you still have it (shown in the app under Settings → Account) so we can locate your record. If you no longer have it, email from the address associated with any purchase and we will help.

Request account deletion by email

Opens your email app with a pre-filled request to team@digitechdynamics.com. Or email us directly at team@digitechdynamics.com.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove the following within 30 days of your request:

DataWhere it lives
Your profile, preferences, stream history, and app-generated user IDOn your device (local app storage)
Your analytics profile and event historyMixpanel, Firebase Analytics
Your customer / subscription recordRevenueCat

What may be retained

A limited amount of data may be kept after deletion where the law requires it or where it is not tied to your identity:

  • Transaction and tax records — purchase records held by Apple, Google, or our payment processor may be retained as required for financial and legal compliance. These are managed by the app stores, not deleted by us.
  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics — data that can no longer be linked to you or your device.
  • Short-lived backups — encrypted backups that auto-expire shortly after deletion.

How long it takes

In-app deletion removes your account and on-device data right away. Remaining data across our systems and processors is fully purged within 30 days. If you request deletion by email, we begin processing as soon as we receive your request and complete it within the same 30-day window.

Questions

For anything related to your data or this process, contact us at team@digitechdynamics.com. For full details on what we collect and how we use it, see our Privacy Policy.
